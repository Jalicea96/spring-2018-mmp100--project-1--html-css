Hideo Kojima

Who is Hideo Kojima

Hideo Kojima is a Japanese video game designer, screenwriter, director, and game producer. he is the founder of Kojima Productions, which he founded in 2005. Hideo also worked for the well-known gaming conglomerate Konami Holding Corporation, where he rose to the position of Vice-president in 2011. Hideo is one of the most talented video game auteurs due to his involvement in almost all aspects of gaming production. Hideo Kojima is responsible for the making of many well-known video games but the video game he is most well known for is the Metal Gear series.
